About Me

Welcome! I'm Ray Shkabari, a dedicated and dynamic software developer with an educational foundation in Mechanical Engineering from Dalhousie University and specialized training in Computer Science from Harvard University and The Odin Project. My technical odyssey commenced with an enthusiasm for mechanical systems, but it wasn't long before I was captivated by the elegance and power of software development. It's here, at the confluence of logic and creativity, where I've found my professional home. I bring a versatile skill set with a command of languages such as Python, Java, C, C++, JavaScript, and TypeScript. With a comprehensive understanding of frameworks and tools like Git, HTML/CSS, MATLAB, React, Node.js, and Flask, I craft full-stack web applications that prioritize both form and function. My experience as a Tech Sales Associate at Staples Canada has imbued me with a robust understanding of IT solutions, where I significantly boosted customer engagement and problem resolution efficiency. These skills translate seamlessly into my ability to communicate intricate technical concepts clearly and effectively. Outside the digital realm, I'm continually immersing myself in the latest technological advancements, contributing to open-source initiatives, or deconstructing hardware to sate my curiosity for how things work. I'm eager to leverage my diverse expertise to contribute to innovative projects and collaborative teams.
