About Me

Welcome! I'm Ray Shkabari, a dedicated and dynamic software developer with an educational foundation in Mechanical Engineering from Dalhousie University and specialized training in Computer Science from Harvard University and The Odin Project.

With a robust skill set that spans essential programming languages like Python, Java, C, C++, JavaScript, TypeScript, and SQL, I excel in creating solutions that are both innovative and effective. My proficiency extends to web development with a solid grasp of HTML/CSS, alongside proficiency in React, Node.js, Flask, and the use of Git for version control.

Beyond programming languages, I have a profound understanding of computer science principles that form the backbone of any great software: from constructing and manipulating complex data structures to optimizing algorithms for peak performance. My analytical prowess is complemented by a thorough understanding of memory management, ensuring that the applications I develop are not only functional but also efficient and scalable.

As a Freelance Web Developer, I immersed myself in the creative process of building responsive and user-friendly websites. My experience spans using a variety of technologies like HTML5, CSS3, React, and Node.js, focusing on crafting engaging and smooth online experiences. A significant part of my work involved optimizing MySQL databases to ensure efficient and seamless website functionality. This journey has been about honing my web development skills and delivering quality solutions that meet diverse client needs.

Outside the digital realm, I'm continually immersing myself in the latest technological advancements, or deconstructing hardware to sate my curiosity for how things work.

I'm eager to leverage my diverse expertise to contribute to innovative projects and collaborative teams.
